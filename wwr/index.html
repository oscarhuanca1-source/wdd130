Pisco

Pisco is a beautiful city located south of Lima, a travel of 3 hours in auto. In my city you can enjoy our seafood dishes like famous ceviche and visit Paracas Nature Reserve in this place you can visit "El Candelabro". The Paracas Candelabra is a massive geoglyph etched into a sandy hillside on the Paracas Peninsula in Pisco city, Ica region of Peru. It is approximately 180 meters long and visible from the sea, making it an enigmatic symbol of the pre-Incan culture.
