Ica, My Favorite City

Ica city is a great place to live, visit and go to vacation, this city is ubicated in the south of Lima the Perú's capital.


In this city you can visit "La Huacachina", It is a unique natural oasis in South America, located in the Ica Desert, Peru, about 4-5 hours from Lima.
Famous for its green lagoon surrounded by high dunes, it is the main destination for sandboarding and sunset dune buggy rides.
